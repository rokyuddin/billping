- **Headline**: "Stop Burning Money on Forgotten Subscriptions."
- **Subheadline**: "The brutally simple tracker for your recurring digital life. Get alerts, track spending, and cancel the noise."
- **CTA**: "Start Tracking - Free" (Primary), "View Live Demo" (Secondary).

1.  **Smart Reminders**: "We ping you before you pay. Never get hit with a surprise renewal again."
2.  **Expense Analytics**: "See where your money bleeds. Visual breakdowns of your monthly burn rate."

4.  **Privacy First**: "Your data is yours. We don't sell it. We just help you save money."

## 2. Pricing Strategy

### Free Plan ($0/mo)

- Track up to 5 subscriptions.
- Basic email reminders (3 days before).
- Monthly spend summary.

### Pro Plan ($5/mo)

- **Unlimited** subscriptions.
- **Custom** reminder schedules (Email & Push).
- Advanced Analytics & Trends.
- Multi-currency support.
- Priority Support.

### Business Plan ($15/mo)

- Team management.
- API Access.
- Export to CSV/PDF.
- Dedicated account manager.

## 3. FAQ

- **Q**: Is my financial data safe?
  - **A**: Yes. We use bank-level encryption and never store your credit card details directly.
- **Q**: Can I import from my bank statement?
  - **A**: Currently supported via CSV import. Direct bank connection coming in Q4.
- **Q**: How do reminders work?
  - **A**: You choose. Email, browser push, or both. Set custom timing (1 day, 3 days, 1 week before).
